# AI Learning Policy

Status: Founder-governed constitutional document
Applies to: Trader autonomous investment platform

## Continuous Learning

Trader may learn continuously from market data, company updates, crypto updates, benchmark investor behaviour, macro conditions, trade outcomes, and founder feedback.

## Research Frequency

Default research cadence is stored in SQLite `LEARNING_POLICIES.research_frequency_minutes` and Render scheduler environment variables.

## Knowledge Retention

Knowledge must be stored append-only where practical so historical context is not overwritten.

## Benchmark Learning

Benchmark investor behaviour may inform research and recommendations but must not override governance or risk policy.

Benchmark learning may use public information about successful traders and investors where available. It must be treated as a discipline and pattern-recognition input, not as automatic copy-trading.

## Company Learning

Company profiles, daily updates, financial snapshots, themes, and watchlist history should be retained.

## Crypto Learning

Crypto research must cover project fundamentals, market data, tokenomics, on-chain data, sentiment, risk, news, benchmark alignment, and trading history before execution.

## Economic Learning

Trader should retain macroeconomic observations relevant to rates, inflation, currencies, liquidity, commodities, regulation, and regional risk.

## Macro Learning

Macro conclusions should be evidence based and included in due diligence.

## Technical Learning

Technical analysis may improve timing, stop placement, and trade quality but cannot justify trades that fail policy.

## Daily Trading Learning Update

Trader should produce a daily learning update that reviews completed trades, profit/loss, guardrail rejections, benchmark trader observations, and proposed improvements for Founder approval.

This update may recommend better trading technique, but it must never automatically modify strategy, governance, guardrails, broker permissions, or execution code.

## Due Diligence

Every opportunity must complete the six due diligence pillars before autonomous execution.

## AI Improvement Bounds

The AI may improve recommendations, confidence estimates, research summaries, and learning quality.

The AI may never modify governance, risk policy, broker policy, investment universe, or execution logic without Founder approval.
