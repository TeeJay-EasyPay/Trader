# Executive Founder Brief - Foundation Sprint

Date: 2026-07-04

Trader has been upgraded from a paper trading assistant toward a governed autonomous investment platform.

## What Changed

- Founder-controlled constitutional governance documents now define investment policy, risk management, broker execution, AI learning, and the approved investment universe.
- SQLite now stores configurable policy tables instead of relying only on environment values.
- The Investment Orchestrator now records due diligence, structured investment scores, broker decisions, execution decisions, and capital allocation history before execution.
- Crypto knowledge tables are ready for real public data without dummy entries.
- Kraken credentials now support the Render `KRAKEN_PRIVATE_KEY` naming while trading remains disabled by policy until Founder approval.
- The mobile app still has only Command, Recommendations, and Intelligence screens, now with broker panels, due diligence status, and structured score visibility.

## Founder Controls

- Governance documents are Founder-only.
- Auto trading remains disabled unless policy and environment explicitly allow it.
- Crypto trading remains disabled by default.
- Live trading remains unapproved.

## Current Recommendation

Run the platform in paper/sandbox mode until several days of continuous due diligence, research runs, and policy-compliant paper decisions have been reviewed.
