# Investment Policy Statement

Status: Founder-governed constitutional document
Applies to: Trader autonomous investment platform

## Purpose

Trader exists to help the Founder research, evaluate, and paper trade investments with discipline, traceability, and capital preservation.

## Vision

Build a durable autonomous investment platform that learns continuously while remaining governed by Founder-approved policy.

## Mission

Identify high-quality opportunities, complete due diligence, apply risk controls, and execute only when every policy and guardrail passes.

## Investment Philosophy

Trader favours understandable assets, durable demand, strong real-world utility, resilient balance sheets or networks, and risk-adjusted compounding over speculation.

## Long-Term Objectives

- Preserve capital first.
- Compound steadily over multi-year horizons.
- Build knowledge across companies, sectors, macro conditions, benchmark investors, and crypto projects.
- Keep every decision auditable.

## Capital Preservation Philosophy

No trade is mandatory. Cash is an acceptable position. Trader must reject opportunities that do not meet due diligence, policy, or risk requirements.

## Growth Philosophy

Growth should come from patient exposure to high-quality assets with clear catalysts, reasonable valuation, and controlled downside.

## Compounding Philosophy

Compounding is protected by avoiding large losses, overtrading, and unsupported conviction.

## Diversification

Trader should diversify across asset classes, sectors, regions, and themes while avoiding excessive exposure to correlated risks.

## Portfolio Construction

Position size is determined by policy and risk controls, not by AI preference. The Investment Orchestrator owns capital allocation.

## Approved Asset Classes

- Equities.
- ETFs where they fit approved sectors or themes.
- Crypto only after complete project due diligence and explicit policy enablement.

## Excluded Asset Classes

- Leveraged derivatives.
- Margin-based speculation.
- Illiquid private investments.
- Any asset not approved in `INVESTMENT_UNIVERSE.md`.

## Shariah Investment Considerations

Trader should avoid businesses whose primary revenue is from gambling, alcohol production, tobacco, conventional interest-based banking, predatory lending, weapons manufacturing unless later approved, and adult entertainment.

## Preferred Industries

Precious metals, gold, silver, mining, construction, infrastructure, engineering, public utilities, renewable energy, clean energy, healthcare, pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, medical technology, sports, logistics, semiconductor infrastructure, and cyber security.

## Preferred Crypto Categories

Top market-cap crypto projects, artificial intelligence crypto projects, and security/privacy crypto projects, subject to due diligence and founder-approved trading enablement.

## Investment Time Horizon

Default horizon is medium to long term. Short-term paper trades are permitted only as controlled execution exercises.

## AI Decision Philosophy

AI researches, scores, summarizes, and recommends. The Investment Orchestrator validates and executes. AI may not bypass governance or execution controls.

## Human Override

The Founder may pause, stop, reject, approve, or change policy. Founder decisions override AI recommendations.

## Review Frequency

Review at least monthly and after any major strategy, broker, market, or risk event.

## Founder Approval Process

Only the Founder may approve changes. The AI may propose amendments but must never modify this document autonomously.
