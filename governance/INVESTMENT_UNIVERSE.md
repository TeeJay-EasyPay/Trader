# Investment Universe

Status: Founder-governed constitutional document
Applies to: Trader autonomous investment platform

## Purpose

The Investment Universe defines which asset classes, industries, sectors, exchanges, brokers, countries, regions, and cryptocurrencies Trader may research and invest in.

Trader may research outside this universe for education, but the Investment Orchestrator may never execute outside the approved universe unless the Founder approves a governance change.

## Approved Asset Classes

- Equities.
- ETFs aligned with approved sectors or themes.
- Crypto projects only after complete due diligence and explicit policy enablement.

## Approved Exchanges

- United Kingdom, European, Asian, African, Australian, and United States exchanges where supported by an approved broker.
- Kraken for crypto only after Founder approval and safe permissions.
- Future exchanges require broker policy approval.

## Approved Brokers

- Alpaca Paper Trading for equities.
- Kraken prepared for crypto research and future execution, disabled by default.
- Coinbase prepared for future support, disabled by default.
- Interactive Brokers and Saxo prepared for future support, disabled by default.

## Approved Countries

United Kingdom, European countries, Asian countries, African countries, Australia, and the United States.

## Approved Regions

Preferred regions are United Kingdom, Europe, Asia, Africa, and Australia. United States equities are permitted but are not the primary investment focus.

## Approved Industries

Precious metals, gold, silver, mining, construction, infrastructure, engineering, public utilities, renewable energy, clean energy, healthcare, pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, medical technology, sports, logistics, semiconductor infrastructure, and cyber security.

## Approved Crypto Categories

- Top 20 cryptocurrencies by market capitalisation.
- Top 20 artificial intelligence cryptocurrencies.
- Top 20 security/privacy cryptocurrencies.

The Crypto Knowledge Engine should continuously update this universe using public, real information.

## Excluded Industries

Trader should avoid investments whose primary business or revenue source is gambling, casinos, betting, alcohol production, tobacco, conventional interest-based banking, predatory lending, weapons manufacturing unless later approved, or adult entertainment.

## Excluded Asset Classes

Leveraged derivatives, unsupported broker products, unapproved crypto projects, and assets that cannot complete due diligence.

## Review Frequency

Review monthly and after material market, broker, regulatory, or founder strategy changes.

## Founder Approval Process

Only the Founder may modify the Investment Universe. The AI may propose changes but must never edit this document autonomously.
